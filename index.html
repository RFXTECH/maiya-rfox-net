✨ Fun Facts About Sprigatito

  • Sprigatito is one of the three starter Pokémon in Pokémon Scarlet & Violet!
  • Its fur is similar to plants, and it can photosynthesize by soaking up sunlight
  • When Sprigatito kneads with its front paws, it releases a sweet aroma that mesmerizes everyone around it
  • It's known as the "Grass Cat Pokémon" — it's basically a magical plant kitty!
  • Sprigatito is 1'4" (0.4m) tall and weighs 9.0 lbs (4.1 kg) — super tiny and cute!
  • Its ability is Overgrow, which powers up Grass-type moves when it's in a tough spot
  • Sprigatito's attention-seeking personality means it gets sulky if its Trainer gives attention to other Pokémon
  • It was introduced in Generation IX and is the Paldea region's Grass-type starter
  • The sweet scent from its fur has therapeutic qualities and can make opponents lose their will to battle
  • Its design is inspired by an Iberian Lynx kitten mixed with a marijuana/catnip plant 🌱
